Strategic Alignment & Problem Definition

Primary Objective: Empower institutional leadership to synchronize academic curricula with real-world workforce demand, pinpointing high-growth potential in emerging sectors.

Target Audience: Curriculum designers, strategic program planners, and institutional leadership.

Value Proposition: Reducing the "job–training mismatch" by providing high-fidelity visibility into structural employment demand and differentiating between static and emerging skill sets.

Data Acquisition & Collection

Gathering comprehensive market intelligence to serve as the project foundation:

  • Primary Dataset: SGJobData (CSV format).
  • Context: Aggregated Singapore-specific job postings, including metadata on vacancies, application volume, and sectoral categories.

Data Preparation & Engineering

Refining raw data into a reliable analytical asset:

  • Data Cleaning: Elimination of empty rows and unused columns; cap average salary using Log-IQR;noise reduction in job titles using fuzzy matching.
  • Data Enrichment: Integration of skills data to enable competency mapping.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Uncovering latent market behaviors through pattern recognition:

  • Trend Identification: Comparative analysis of job vacancies against application volume across diverse economic sectors.
  • Sectoral Profiling: Identifying demand velocity and role-specific density.
